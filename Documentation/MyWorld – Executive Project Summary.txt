MyWorld – Executive Project Summary

Based on the project documentation provided, the application has evolved from a simple Real Estate CRM into a multi-tenant SaaS Real Estate Intelligence Platform that combines CRM, WhatsApp automation, property matching, lead management, and AI-driven data extraction.

1. Project Objective

The primary objective of MyWorld is to create a mobile-friendly, multi-tenant SaaS platform for real estate agencies that can:

Manage buyers, properties, visits, executives, and deals.
Automatically assign and track leads.
Capture real estate information from WhatsApp groups.
Convert unstructured WhatsApp conversations into structured business data.
Match buyers with suitable properties automatically.
Improve executive productivity and conversion rates.
Allow multiple agencies to operate securely on the same platform while maintaining complete tenant isolation.
Ultimately become a Real Estate Intelligence Platform rather than a traditional CRM.
